The Artist

Seven years of turning vision into image — one frame, one stroke, one story at a time.

I am Timilehin Taiwo, a multidisciplinary visual artist based in Lagos, Nigeria. I work across storyboard art, digital portrait painting, album cover illustration, and graphic design, with a focus on storytelling and strong visual narrative.

What started as a passion for portraiture has grown into a broader creative practice, especially in storyboard and Video pre-production, where I think in motion and sequence, not just still images. My work sits somewhere between commercial clarity and personal expression, depending on what the project demands.

I approach every project as a collaboration, not just a commission.

How It Works

01

Brief

You share the project — a short description, reference images if any, your timeline and budget. No rigid forms. A message is enough.

02

Scope & Quote

The project gets scoped properly — deliverables, format, turnaround time — and a clear quote is sent. A deposit confirms the commission.

03

Creation

Work begins. Depending on the project, you may see rough drafts, sketches, or frame layouts for review before the final render.

04

Delivery

Final files are delivered in agreed formats — high-res softcopy and, for portrait prints, physical delivery within Lagos or by arrangement.

05

Commission Work

Have a project in mind? Let's make it.

Whether you're directing a short film and need a storyboard, gifting someone a portrait, dropping an EP and need cover art, or building a brand — reach out. Every project starts with a conversation.
